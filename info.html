Overview

From inspiring rescued marine mammals to colorful clownfish, the Aquarium is home to more than 120 species of animals representing a wide array of ecosystems. Take some time to get to know our aquatic ambassadors and immerse yourself in their incredible stories.

Penguin

Penguins are adorable flightless birds that are highly adapted to life in the water, with their sleek bodies and waterproof feathers.

Marine mammal

Marine mammals have evolved to survive in aquatic environments, relying on their unique adaptations to navigate and hunt underwater.

Fish & invertebrate

The ocean is home to a variety of fish and invertebrates, from clownfish and corals to octopuses and sea stars, demonstrating the diversity of life beneath the waves.
