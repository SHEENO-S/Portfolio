Monthly giving

An automatic, monthly donation is the easiest, most sustainable way to help your friends at AquaLand. Become part of a dedicated group of supporters who help carry out our mission to inspire people to make a difference for aquatic life. Your monthly commitment helps us provide optimal care for our animals, educate our local, national, and international visitors, and promote conservation initiatives.

One-time donation

No gift is too small to make a difference for our animals!

  • $15 feeds a clownfish for a year.
  • $25 feeds a seal for a day.
  • $100 feeds a sea lion for a week.
  • $250 feeds a penguin for two months.
  • $500 provides vitamins and medications for our animals for one month.
